Structure

Tense (+) Positive (-) Negative (?) Question
Simple PresentS + V1S + do/does + not + V1Do/Does + S + V1?
Present ContinuousS + am/is/are + V-ingS + am/is/are + not + V-ingAm/Is/Are + S + V-ing?
Present PerfectS + has/have + V3S + has/have + not + V3Has/Have + S + V3?
Present Perfect ContinuousS + has/have been + V-ingS + has/have not been + V-ingHas/Have + S + been + V-ing?

Examples

Tense (+) Positive (-) Negative (?) Question
Simple Present She visits the museum. She does not visit the museum. Does she visit the museum?
Present Continuous They are watching a movie. They are not watching a movie. Are they watching a movie?
Present Perfect He has finished his homework. He has not finished his homework. Has he finished his homework?
Present Perfect Continuous I have been studying for two hours. I have not been studying for two hours. Have I been studying for two hours?

Present Perfect Continuous
