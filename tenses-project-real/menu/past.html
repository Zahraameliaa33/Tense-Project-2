Structure

Tense (+) Positive (-) Negative (?) Question
Simple PastS + V2S + did + not + V1Did + S + V1?
Past ContinuousS + was/were + V-ingS + was/were + not + V-ingWas/Were + S + V-ing?
Past PerfectS + had + V3S + had not + V3Had + S + V3?
Past Perfect ContinuousS + had been + V-ingS + had not been + V-ingHad + S + been + V-ing?

Examples

Tense (+) Positive (-) Negative (?) Question
Simple Past She visited the museum. She did not visit the museum. Did she visit the museum?
Past Continuous They were watching a movie. They were not watching a movie. Were they watching a movie?
Past Perfect He had finished his homework. He had not finished his homework. Had he finished his homework?
Past Perfect Continuous I had been studying for two hours. I had not been studying for two hours. Had I been studying for two hours?
