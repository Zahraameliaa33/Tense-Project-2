Structure

Tense (+) Positive (-) Negative (?) Question
Simple FutureS + will + V1S + will not + V1Will + S + V1?
Future ContinuousS + will be + V-ingS + will not be + V-ingWill + S + be + V-ing?
Future PerfectS + will have + V3S + will not have + V3Will + S + have + V3?
Future Perfect ContinuousS + will have been + V-ingS + will not have been + V-ingWill + S + have been + V-ing?

Examples

Tense (+) Positive (-) Negative (?) Question
Simple Future She will visit the museum. She will not visit the museum. Will she visit the museum?
Future Continuous They will be watching a movie. They will not be watching a movie. Will they be watching a movie?
Future Perfect He will have finished his homework. He will not have finished his homework. Will he have finished his homework?
Future Perfect Continuous I will have been studying for two hours. I will not have been studying for two hours. Will I have been studying for two hours?

Simple Future: Use for actions that will happen in the future.
Future Continuous: Use for actions that will be ongoing at a specific time in the future.
Future Perfect: Use for actions that will be completed before a certain time in the future.
Future Perfect Continuous: Use to show the duration of an action up to a certain point in the future.
