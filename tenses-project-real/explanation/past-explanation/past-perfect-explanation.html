Quick difference (easy)

Simple Past: one finished action (I ate dinner).
Past Continuous: action in progress (I was eating dinner at 7pm).
Past Perfect: action completed before another past action (I had eaten before he arrived).
Past Perfect Continuous: action that was ongoing for some time before another past action (I had been eating for 30 minutes before he arrived).
