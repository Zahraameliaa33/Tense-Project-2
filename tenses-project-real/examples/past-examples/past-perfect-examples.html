Past Perfect Examples

  • (+) I had finished my homework before dinner.
  • (-) I had not finished my homework before dinner.
  • (?) Had I finished my homework before dinner?
  • (+) You had visited the museum before it closed.
  • (-) You had not visited the museum before it closed.
  • (?) Had you visited the museum before it closed?
  • (+) They had left the party when I arrived.
  • (-) They had not left the party when I arrived.
  • (?) Had they left the party when I arrived?
  • (+) We had eaten before the movie started.
  • (-) We had not eaten before the movie started.
  • (?) Had we eaten before the movie started?
  • (+) She had called her friend before leaving.
  • (-) She had not called her friend before leaving.
  • (?) Had she called her friend before leaving?
  • (+) He had completed the project by Friday.
  • (-) He had not completed the project by Friday.
  • (?) Had he completed the project by Friday?
  • (+) It had stopped raining before we left.
  • (-) It had not stopped raining before we left.
  • (?) Had it stopped raining before we left?
